Events

The Institute Technical Council (ITC) at IIT Bombay orchestrates a diverse array of events aimed at fostering technical innovation and collaboration among students. These events span workshops, competitions, exhibitions, and symposiums, providing platforms for students to showcase their talents and engage with the broader technological community.

Tech Connect 2024

Held from December 17th to 19th, 2024, Tech Connect 2024 was a premier research and development outreach event. Inaugurated by Dr. Samir Kamat, Secretary of DDR&D and Chairman of DRDO, the event showcased IIT Bombay's research innovations to the public, including students, industries, and academia. Dr. Kamat delivered a talk on "Defense R&D - Challenges & Opportunities," emphasizing collaborative initiatives and encouraging young minds to pursue careers in defense research. The event featured technology showcases, scientific models, interactive games, and laboratory prototypes, facilitating knowledge exchange and collaboration.

Inter IIT Tech Meet 13.0

From December 11th to 14th, 2024, IIT Bombay hosted the 13th edition of the Inter IIT Tech Meet, themed "The Timeless Technopolis." The event brought together tech enthusiasts from various IITs to tackle problem statements in areas like product development, quantitative finance, consulting, and robotics. Highlights included the Tech & RnD Expo, showcasing innovations such as motorsports cars and drone technology, and offering networking opportunities. The meet concluded with a concert by Dream Note and a networking dinner, fostering collaboration across campuses.

Institute Colloquium Series

The Institute Colloquium is a prestigious lecture series that invites pioneers, global leaders, and renowned scholars to share their insights with the IIT Bombay community. Aimed at promoting intellectual discourse and expanding knowledge horizons, the colloquium has hosted distinguished speakers such as Nobel Laureates Prof. Serge Haroche and Prof. Joachim Frank, and former Vice-Chairperson of NITI Aayog, Prof. Arvind Panagariya. These sessions provide a forum for high-level discussions across various disciplines, enriching the academic experience of students and faculty alike.

Aeromodelling Club Events

The Aeromodelling Club, under ITC, conducts various events to promote aviation and aeromodelling among students. Notable events include the RC Plane Competition, which attracts over 400 students forming 109 teams to design, build, and fly remote-controlled planes. Other activities like the PlutoX Drone Hackathon and Glider Workshops provide hands-on experience in drone technology and glider construction, fostering practical skills and innovation.

Techfest

Techfest, IIT Bombay's annual science and technology festival, is Asia's largest, attracting over 175,000 attendees and hosting more than 300 events. Established with the motto of promoting science and technology, Techfest provides an international platform for youth to showcase their talents through competitions, workshops, and exhibitions. The festival has featured renowned personalities like Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam and astronaut Dr. Rakesh Sharma, inspiring students to pursue technological solutions to real-life problems. ​

Through these events, ITC continues to cultivate a culture of technical excellence and innovation, providing students with opportunities to develop their skills, collaborate across disciplines, and contribute to technological advancements.​
