About Us

The 'T' in IIT Bombay is right here

The Institute Technical Council (ITC) at IIT Bombay is dedicated to fostering a vibrant culture of innovation, technical proficiency, and leadership among students. Serving as the central hub for all technical activities within the institute, ITC provides a comprehensive platform that encompasses various technical clubs, teams, and communities. This structure ensures that students have access to the necessary resources and mentorship to transform their innovative ideas into tangible projects. ITC's mission revolves around promoting interdisciplinary collaboration and creativity by organizing an array of workshops, competitions, and projects across diverse technical fields. These initiatives are designed to enhance the practical skills of students, encouraging them to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world challenges. By facilitating such engagements, ITC plays a pivotal role in preparing students for future technological advancements and leadership roles. The council boasts an impressive network, comprising over 20 technical bodies, including clubs, tech teams, and communities. Annually, ITC orchestrates more than 80 events and oversees over 200 summer projects, effectively catering to the technical aspirations of over 10,000 students.
